AI-powered product matching and MAP compliance monitoring

The Problem

Brands were losing millions to price erosion and MAP violations across marketplaces. Unauthorized sellers, grey market activities, and price compression were impossible to track manually across 150+ platforms. Brands couldn't identify where price drops originated or quantify business impact.

Strategic Approach

Led development of our flagship pricing intelligence product—the highest-revenue generator in our portfolio. Built AI-powered product matching algorithm, designed scraping infrastructure, and created analytics showing "who moved first" in price changes.

Technical Architecture

  • AI product matching across marketplaces despite title/image variations
  • Web scraping infrastructure covering 150+ marketplaces with multiple daily scrapes
  • Data pipeline handling millions of price points with currency normalization
  • Causality algorithms detecting price drop initiation and cross-platform impact
  • ZIP code clustering for location-based pricing intelligence (innovation)

Innovation Highlights

  • First-mover detection showing which marketplace/seller initiated price drops
  • Cross-platform impact analysis quantifying revenue loss
